Ms. Aditi Kumari

Senior Executive & Strategic Leader
A dedicated and accomplished professional with expertise in strategic planning and operational excellence, Ms. Aditi Kumari exemplifies a modern leadership style rooted in innovation, empathy, and efficiency. Her approach to leadership blends analytical acumen with creative problem-solving, enabling her to drive impactful outcomes across the organization. With a strong educational foundation—a Bachelor’s in Computer Applications (BCA) from Magadh University and an MBA in Rural Development from William Carey University—Ms. Aditi offers a rare blend of technical proficiency and a deep understanding of community-driven growth. As Director of MetConnect Infotech Pvt. Ltd., she plays a central role in shaping the company’s people-first culture, business strategy, and long-term vision. Her focus on employee development, process optimization, and policy innovation has elevated workplace efficiency and morale. She also plays a key role in bridging the HR and IT verticals to ensure seamless collaboration between teams. Ms. Aditi is equally committed to social impact initiatives. As a core leader of the Manti Foundation, she advocates for education, gender equality, and rural upliftment, taking a hands-on approach to projects that empower underserved communities. Her leadership is marked by transparency, adaptability, and a deep sense of purpose. Ms. Aditi continues to inspire teams and stakeholders alike through her unwavering commitment to progress, integrity, and inclusive growth.

🎯 Leadership Philosophy

Empowering teams through collaborative leadership, driving innovation through strategic thinking, and creating sustainable value through operational excellence and forward-thinking approaches.
