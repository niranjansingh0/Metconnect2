Accessibility Statement

At Metconnect Infotech, we are deeply committed to creating an inclusive digital environment that empowers every user, regardless of their abilities or assistive needs.

Inclusive Design Principles

Our platforms are crafted with accessibility at the core—ensuring compatibility with screen readers, keyboard-only navigation, voice recognition software, and other assistive technologies.

Clear Text & Visual Standards

We use high-contrast colors, scalable fonts, and descriptive alternative text for all meaningful images and media to support users with low vision or visual impairments.

Mobile-Friendly & Responsive

Our websites and applications are fully responsive, maintaining accessibility features and usability across smartphones, tablets, and desktops of various screen sizes.

WCAG 2.1 Compliance

We follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 to meet and exceed industry standards for accessibility, and we strive for AA or higher compliance wherever feasible.

Ongoing Testing & Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing process. We conduct periodic audits, usability tests, and user feedback evaluations to continuously improve the inclusivity of our solutions.

Support & Assistance

If you face any accessibility issues while using our website or services, please contact us at support@metconnect.in. Our team is ready to assist you promptly and effectively.

We Value Your Feedback

Your suggestions and experiences help us make our platform better for everyone. Please feel free to share any accessibility feedback or recommendations with us.
