About Our Company

Metconnect Infotech Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted name in the field of HR outsourcing, legal compliance, and IT solutions. Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between people and technology, we have grown into a dynamic partner for businesses looking to scale efficiently and compliantly. With our headquarters in Patna, Bihar, we are proud to support clients across India in achieving operational excellence and digital transformation.

We specialize in providing end-to-end workforce management solutions, custom software development, and statutory compliance services. Our team consists of industry experts, HR professionals, software engineers, and legal advisors who work together to deliver high-impact, cost-effective solutions. From small startups to large enterprises, we tailor our offerings to meet the specific needs of our clients with flexibility and precision.
